Hike to Potato Chip Rock

The hike to potato chip rock is a long hike that takes about 4-5 hours to complete. Bring lots of water because it can get really hot. There will be a big line at the top, and you will need to jump from one rock to another to get to the potatochip rock. When you see the antennas at the top, that means you are close.
